Thousands of people near you tonight. Gigs, openings, run clubs, quiz nights, last-minute deals, all within walking distance. ## How it works

1. **See what's on** — Open Lincc and instantly see everything happening around you: events, deals, openings, meetups and more.
2. **Find your vibe** — Filter by time, place or interest. From tonight's plans to this weekend's events.
3. **Get out there** — Grab a deal, join an event or try something new. Everything you need to turn a quiet night into a good one.

- **New things to do** — Pop-up markets, open mic nights, food festivals, club openings. Discover things you never knew were happening.
