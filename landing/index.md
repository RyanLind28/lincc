# Lincc

> Everything happening around you, in one place.

**No Algorithms. Just what's happening now.**

Discover local events, deals, and things to do near you.

Tired of Instagram showing you last week's posts when you want to know what's on tonight? Lincc is different. Everything you see is happening now or soon, in the order it was posted. No algorithms choosing for you.

## Why Lincc

Real-time. Chronological. Unfiltered. Your social life, sorted.

## How it works

1. **See what's on** — Open Lincc and instantly see everything happening around you: events, deals, openings, meetups, and more.
2. **Find your vibe** — Narrow it down by time, place, or interest. From tonight's plans to this weekend's events.
3. **Get out there** — Grab a deal, join an event, or try something new. Everything you need to go is right here.

## Features

- **New connections** — Find a coffee buddy, a running partner, a co-working crew, or a whole new circle.
- **New things to do** — Pop-up markets, open mic nights, food festivals, club openings — discover things you never knew were happening.
- **Exclusive deals** — Offers and discounts from local restaurants, bars, and shops, only on Lincc.
- **Always something on** — Live, real-time events updated around the clock.
- **Right on your doorstep** — See what's within walking distance or explore further out.
- **Support local** — Discover independent restaurants, shops, and venues in your neighbourhood.

## For Businesses

Reach customers when it matters. Whether you're a restaurant, bar, shop, or venue, Lincc puts your events, offers, and promotions in front of people who are nearby and ready to go.

- Post events, offers & promotions instantly
- Reach people within walking distance
- Drive foot traffic with time-limited deals
- Track engagement and redemptions
