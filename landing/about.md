# About Lincc

> We're building your local pulse, one place to discover everything happening around you.

We started Lincc because finding out what's happening around you should not be this hard. You live in a city full of people and plans, yet finding any of it means trawling Instagram, Google and five different group chats, and you still miss half of it. There had to be a better way. We are rolling out city by city.

Lincc brings everything together in one place. Events, deals, openings, happenings — all live, all local, all at your fingertips. Whether it's a nightclub opening, a restaurant running a limited-time offer, a pop-up market, or a community event — if it's happening near you, it's on Lincc.

We're not just another events app. We're building the go-to destination for discovering your area — a real-time feed of everything worth knowing about, filtered to exactly when and where you want. No endless scrolling. No searching for hours. Just open the app and see what's on.

## What we believe

- **Real-time or nothing** — Stale information is useless. Everything on Lincc is live and current. If it's expired, it's gone. You only see what's actually happening.
- **Local first** — Your area is full of amazing things — you just don't know about them yet. Lincc surfaces what's nearby so you can stop searching and start exploring.
- **Everything in one place** — No more jumping between apps, websites, and social feeds. One app for events, deals, openings, and everything else worth knowing about.
- **Trust & quality** — Verified businesses and vetted listings mean you can trust what you see. No spam, no clickbait — just genuine events and offers.
