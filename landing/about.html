About Us

We're building your
local pulse

A city full of people, and somehow never anything to do. We are out to fix that, starting with Sheffield.

We started Lincc because finding out what's happening around you should not be this hard. You live in a city full of people and plans, yet finding any of it means trawling Instagram, Google and five different group chats, and you still miss half of it. There had to be a better way.

Lincc brings everything together in one place. Events, deals, openings and happenings, all live, all local, all at your fingertips. Whether it is a nightclub opening, a restaurant running a limited-time offer, a pop-up market or a community run club, if it is happening near you, it is on Lincc.

We are not just another events app. We are building the go-to place for discovering your area, a real-time feed of everything worth leaving the house for, filtered to exactly when and where you want. No endless scrolling, no searching for hours. Just open the app and see what's on. We are launching in Sheffield first, then rolling out city by city.

What we believe

Real-time or nothing

Stale information is useless. Everything on Lincc is live and current — if it's expired, it's gone. You only see what's actually happening.

Local first

Your area is full of amazing things — you just don't know about them yet. Lincc surfaces what's nearby so you can stop searching and start exploring.

Everything in one place

No more jumping between apps, websites, and social feeds. One app for events, deals, openings, and everything else worth knowing about.

Trust & quality

Verified businesses and vetted listings mean you can trust what you see. No spam, no clickbait — just genuine events and offers.
