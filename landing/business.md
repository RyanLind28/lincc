# Lincc for Business

> Be where Sheffield is looking. Reach nearby customers for a fraction of the cost of traditional advertising.

Lincc is a brand new app putting local events, deals and openings in front of people nearby, right when they are deciding what to do. Get real eyes on your business, fill your quiet hours and launch a deal in seconds.

[Become a Founding Business](https://www.lincc.live/business#biz-waitlist) — Free to join the waitlist. Founding Sheffield businesses get first pick and early perks.

## Why Lincc works

- **Hyper-local reach** — Your offer lands in front of people within walking distance, not a faceless feed scattered across the country.
- **In the moment** — People open Lincc when they are deciding what to do right now, so you catch them at the exact moment they are ready to go.
- **A fraction of the cost** — Forget pricey ad campaigns and flyers nobody reads. Post a deal or an event in seconds and reach your neighbourhood for less.
- **Get in early** — Lincc is new and growing fast. Founding businesses get featured first and build a head start while the city is just discovering us.

## What you can post

- **Flash deals and offers** — Push a time-limited discount and bring nearby people in. Live in seconds, gone when it ends.
- **Vouchers** — Create redeemable vouchers with their own codes and track claims and redemptions.
- **Events and openings** — Launch nights, tastings, live music, grand openings.
- **Your business profile** — A verified profile with your logo, location and opening hours.
- **Insights** — Track views and redemptions so you know which offers pull people in.
- **Fill quiet hours** — Target slow afternoons and midweek lulls with offers timed to exactly when you need the footfall.
