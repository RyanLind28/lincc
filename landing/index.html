The honest bit

Crowded city. Empty calendar.

Thousands of people near you tonight. Gigs, openings, run clubs, quiz nights, last-minute deals, all within walking distance. And somehow you are still on the sofa wondering what to do, because finding any of it means trawling five apps and a dozen group chats.

Lincc puts it all in one place, sorted by what's on now, not by what an algorithm wants you to see. Open it, see what's happening near you, go.

And it works both ways. You can post your own plans too, so whatever you are after, someone nearby is up for it as well. Want a gym buddy, just ask. Fancy a hike this weekend, see who else wants to come along. Coffee, five-a-side, a gallery trip, put it out there and find your people.
